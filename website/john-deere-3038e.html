John Deere 3038E Compact Tractor

Product Description

The John Deere 3038E Compact Tractor is an economical and reliable workhorse designed for value-conscious customers. With 38 horsepower and essential features, this tractor provides excellent performance for property maintenance, landscaping, and light agricultural tasks.

Built with John Deere's commitment to quality and backed by their extensive dealer network, the 3038E offers straightforward operation, proven reliability, and excellent value for money. It's the perfect choice for those who need dependable performance without unnecessary complexity.

Engine & Performance Specifications

Engine

  • Yanmar 3-cylinder diesel engine
  • 38 horsepower @ 2,600 RPM
  • 1.6 liter displacement
  • Liquid-cooled with radiator
  • Fuel tank capacity: 10.6 gallons

Transmission

  • 8F/8R PowrReverser transmission
  • Synchronized shuttle shift
  • Wet clutch for smooth operation
  • Four speed ranges

Hydraulics

  • Open center hydraulic system
  • 6.2 GPM hydraulic flow
  • 2,000 PSI system pressure
  • Position control for implements

Three-Point Hitch

  • Category 1 three-point hitch
  • 540 RPM rear PTO
  • Lift capacity: 1,200 lbs at 24" behind lift points
  • Position control with draft sensing

Dimensions & Comfort Features

Dimensions

  • Length: 108.7 inches
  • Width: 50.8 inches
  • Height: 89.0 inches (to ROPS)
  • Wheelbase: 65.4 inches
  • Ground clearance: 10.2 inches
  • Operating weight: 2,545 lbs

Operator Comfort

  • Comfortable suspension seat
  • Adjustable steering wheel
  • Easy-to-read analog gauges
  • Convenient control layout
  • Foldable ROPS for low clearance areas
  • Rubber floor mat for comfort

Available Attachments & Implements

The 3038E is compatible with a wide range of John Deere attachments and implements:

  • Mower Decks: 60" and 72" mid-mount mower decks
  • Front-End Loader: D160 front-end loader with pin-on bucket
  • Backhoe: 46 backhoe attachment for digging projects
  • Snow Removal: Front blade, rear blade
  • Ground Engaging: Box blade, disc harrow, cultivator
  • Material Handling: Carry-all, landscape rake
