John Deere 1025R Sub-compact Tractor

Product Description

The John Deere 1025R Sub-compact Tractor is the perfect solution for homeowners and small property managers who need reliable, versatile equipment. With 25 horsepower and a compact design, this tractor can handle a wide variety of tasks from mowing and landscaping to light agricultural work and property maintenance.

Built with John Deere's legendary quality and backed by their extensive dealer network, the 1025R combines power, comfort, and ease of use in a size that's perfect for smaller properties and tight spaces.

Engine & Performance Specifications

Engine

  • Yanmar 3-cylinder diesel engine
  • 25 horsepower @ 3,000 RPM
  • 1.2 liter displacement
  • Liquid-cooled with radiator
  • Fuel tank capacity: 7.0 gallons

Transmission

  • Hydrostatic transmission
  • Twin Touch pedals for easy operation
  • Infinite speed control forward and reverse
  • Cruise control available

Hydraulics

  • Open center hydraulic system
  • 5.8 GPM hydraulic flow
  • 2,030 PSI system pressure
  • Position control for implements

Three-Point Hitch

  • Category 1 three-point hitch
  • 540 RPM rear PTO
  • Lift capacity: 680 lbs at 24" behind lift points
  • Draft control and position control

Dimensions & Comfort Features

Dimensions

  • Length: 100.6 inches
  • Width: 47.6 inches
  • Height: 85.4 inches (to ROPS)
  • Wheelbase: 61.4 inches
  • Ground clearance: 7.5 inches
  • Operating weight: 1,477 lbs

Operator Comfort

  • Comfortable 15-inch seat with adjustable suspension
  • Tilt steering wheel
  • Easy-to-read instrument panel
  • Cup holder and storage compartment
  • Foldable ROPS for low clearance areas
  • Automotive-style hood for easy engine access

Available Attachments & Implements

The 1025R is compatible with a wide range of John Deere attachments and implements:

  • Mower Decks: 54" and 60" AutoConnect mid-mount mower decks
  • Front-End Loader: H120 front-end loader with quick-attach bucket
  • Backhoe: 260 backhoe attachment for digging projects
  • Snow Removal: Front blade, snow blower, cab with heat
  • Ground Engaging: Box blade, disc harrow, cultivator, tiller
  • Material Handling: Rear blade, carry-all, pallet forks
