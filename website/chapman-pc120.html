Chapman PC120 Power Collector

Product Description

The Chapman PC120 Power Collector is an innovative grass and leaf collection system designed for ATVs and compact tractors. With a 48-inch collection width and powerful vacuum system, this collector efficiently gathers grass clippings, leaves, and light debris, making lawn maintenance faster and more professional.

Featuring a high-capacity collection hopper and powerful turbine fan, the PC120 delivers exceptional suction power while maintaining excellent maneuverability. It's the ideal solution for maintaining pristine lawns, parks, sports fields, and commercial properties.

Key Features & Specifications

Collection Performance

  • Collection width: 48 inches (120cm)
  • Hopper capacity: 16 cubic feet
  • Turbine fan diameter: 20 inches
  • Fan speed: 3,600 RPM
  • Suction power: 850 CFM

Construction

  • Heavy-duty steel frame construction
  • Reinforced collection deck
  • High-impact plastic hopper
  • Stainless steel turbine fan
  • Powder-coated finish

Power Requirements

  • Minimum tractor PTO: 18 HP
  • PTO speed: 540 RPM
  • Universal joint driveshaft included
  • Slip clutch protection

Dimensions & Weight

  • Overall width: 52 inches
  • Overall length: 72 inches
  • Height: 48 inches
  • Weight: 385 lbs
  • Three-point hitch: Category 1

Design Features

Collection System

  • Floating collection deck follows ground contours
  • Adjustable collection height
  • Anti-scalp wheels
  • Full-width brush strip
  • Side discharge option

Operational Features

  • Hydraulic hopper dump (optional)
  • Manual dump lever (standard)
  • Quick-release hopper
  • Mesh hopper top for visibility
  • Transport wheels for easy movement

Applications

The PC120 excels at:

  • Lawn Maintenance: Collect grass clippings for a pristine finish
  • Leaf Collection: Efficiently gather fall leaves
  • Sports Fields: Maintain professional-quality playing surfaces
  • Parks & Recreation: Keep public spaces clean and attractive
  • Commercial Properties: Professional results for business landscapes
  • Estate Management: Maintain large residential properties
