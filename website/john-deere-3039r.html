John Deere 3039R Compact Tractor

Product Description

The John Deere 3039R Compact Tractor combines premium features with exceptional power and performance. With 39 horsepower and advanced technology, this tractor is perfect for demanding property maintenance, landscaping, hobby farming, and commercial applications.

Featuring a turbocharged engine, premium operator station, and compatibility with John Deere's most advanced attachments, the 3039R delivers the perfect balance of power, comfort, and versatility for operators who demand the best.

Engine & Performance Specifications

Engine

  • Yanmar 3-cylinder turbocharged diesel engine
  • 39 horsepower @ 3,000 RPM
  • 1.6 liter displacement
  • Liquid-cooled with radiator
  • Fuel tank capacity: 10.6 gallons

Transmission

  • eHydro hydrostatic transmission
  • Twin Touch pedals with LoadMatch
  • Infinite speed control forward and reverse
  • Cruise control and MotionMatch

Hydraulics

  • Open center hydraulic system
  • 8.0 GPM hydraulic flow
  • 2,250 PSI system pressure
  • Position control with float function

Three-Point Hitch

  • Category 1 three-point hitch
  • 540 RPM rear PTO
  • Lift capacity: 1,407 lbs at 24" behind lift points
  • iMatch Quick-Hitch compatible

Dimensions & Comfort Features

Dimensions

  • Length: 108.7 inches
  • Width: 50.8 inches
  • Height: 89.0 inches (to ROPS)
  • Wheelbase: 65.4 inches
  • Ground clearance: 10.2 inches
  • Operating weight: 2,315 lbs

Operator Comfort

  • Premium adjustable seat with armrests
  • Tilt steering wheel with comfort grip
  • Digital instrument panel with diagnostics
  • Ergonomic control layout
  • Foldable ROPS for low clearance areas
  • Optional factory-installed cab with HVAC

Available Attachments & Implements

The 3039R is compatible with John Deere's premium attachments and implements:

  • Mower Decks: 54", 60", and 72" AutoConnect mid-mount mower decks
  • Front-End Loader: H165 front-end loader with quick-attach bucket
  • Backhoe: 260B backhoe attachment with thumb option
  • Snow Removal: Front blade, snow blower, cab with heat
  • Ground Engaging: Box blade, disc harrow, cultivator, rotary tiller
  • Material Handling: Rear blade, carry-all, pallet forks, grapple
