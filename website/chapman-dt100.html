Chapman DT100 Hydraulic Dump Trailer

Product Description

The Chapman DT100 Hydraulic Dump Trailer is a heavy-duty, compact trailer designed specifically for ATVs and compact tractors. With a 1-ton capacity and hydraulic dumping mechanism, this trailer makes material handling effortless, whether you're moving soil, gravel, firewood, or garden waste.

Built with Chapman's commitment to durability and functionality, the DT100 features robust construction, smooth hydraulic operation, and excellent maneuverability. It's the perfect companion for property maintenance, landscaping projects, and agricultural work where efficient material transport is essential.

Key Features & Specifications

Capacity & Dimensions

  • Load capacity: 2,000 lbs (1 ton)
  • Box dimensions: 60" L x 40" W x 12" H
  • Cubic capacity: 16.7 cubic feet
  • Overall length: 84 inches
  • Overall width: 48 inches

Construction

  • Heavy-duty steel frame and bed
  • 3mm steel plate bed construction
  • Reinforced tailgate with automatic latches
  • Removable side extensions available
  • Powder-coated finish for durability

Hydraulic System

  • 12V DC hydraulic power unit
  • Single-acting hydraulic cylinder
  • 45-degree maximum dump angle
  • Wireless remote control included
  • Manual override pump for safety

Running Gear

  • Heavy-duty axle with leaf springs
  • 20x10-8 flotation tires
  • Swivel hitch for excellent maneuverability
  • Safety chains included
  • Empty weight: 420 lbs

Design Features

Operational Features

  • Wireless remote control for safe operation
  • Automatic tailgate latches
  • Low loading height for easy filling
  • Tie-down points for securing loads
  • Reflectors and safety markings

Versatility Options

  • Optional mesh sides for volume loads
  • Optional solid side extensions
  • Tool holder attachments
  • Tarp kit for weather protection
  • LED light kit available

Applications

The DT100 is perfect for:

  • Landscaping: Transport mulch, soil, gravel, and decorative stone
  • Property Maintenance: Move debris, leaves, and yard waste
  • Firewood Handling: Efficiently transport split wood
  • Construction: Haul tools, materials, and debris
  • Agricultural Use: Move feed, fertilizer, and harvest produce
  • Snow Removal: Transport sand and salt for winter maintenance
