Chapman FM150 ATV Flail Mower

Product Description

The Chapman FM150 ATV Flail Mower is the larger sibling to the FM120, offering a wider 60-inch cutting width for increased productivity. This heavy-duty flail mower is designed for larger ATVs and UTVs, providing professional-grade performance for extensive property maintenance, commercial landscaping, and agricultural applications.

With its robust construction and powerful cutting capability, the FM150 can handle the toughest vegetation including thick brush, dense weeds, and saplings up to 2.5 inches in diameter. It's the ideal choice for those who need maximum cutting capacity and efficiency from their ATV equipment.

Key Features & Specifications

Cutting Performance

  • Cutting width: 60 inches (150cm)
  • Number of flails: 40 heavy-duty hammers
  • Cutting height: Adjustable from 1" to 4"
  • Maximum cutting capacity: Up to 2.5" diameter saplings
  • Rotor speed: 2,100 RPM

Construction

  • Extra heavy-duty steel deck construction
  • Reinforced cutting chamber with wear plates
  • Replaceable hardened steel flail hammers
  • Heavy-duty front and rear chain guards
  • Premium powder-coated finish

Power Requirements

  • Minimum ATV engine: 500cc recommended
  • PTO driven via heavy-duty universal joint shaft
  • 540 RPM PTO speed
  • Heavy-duty overrunning clutch included

Dimensions & Weight

  • Overall width: 64 inches
  • Transport width: 60 inches
  • Weight: 485 lbs
  • Three-point hitch: Category 1

Design Features

Safety Features

  • Extra heavy-duty front and rear chain guards
  • Reinforced safety deflector chains
  • Heavy-duty overrunning clutch
  • CE approved safety standards
  • Comprehensive safety decals and instructions

Operational Features

  • Height adjustment via heavy-duty skids
  • Offset capability for fence line mowing
  • Self-cleaning deck design
  • Easy access panels for maintenance
  • Remote grease points for all bearings

Applications

The FM150 excels in demanding applications:

  • Large Property Maintenance: Efficiently maintain extensive acreage
  • Commercial Landscaping: Professional results for commercial properties
  • Agricultural Use: Manage crop stubble and field edges
  • Heavy Brush Clearing: Tackle dense vegetation and larger saplings
  • Municipal Work: Maintain parks, trails, and public spaces
  • Forestry Management: Clear undergrowth in wooded areas
