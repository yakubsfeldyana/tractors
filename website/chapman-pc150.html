Chapman PC150 Power Collector

Product Description

The Chapman PC150 Power Collector is the ultimate grass and leaf collection system, offering a massive 60-inch collection width for maximum productivity. Designed for larger tractors and commercial operations, this heavy-duty collector combines exceptional suction power with high-capacity collection for professional groundskeeping.

With its industrial-grade construction and powerful twin-turbine system, the PC150 can handle the most demanding collection tasks. It's the perfect choice for golf courses, sports complexes, municipal parks, and large commercial properties that require efficient, professional-grade maintenance equipment.

Key Features & Specifications

Collection Performance

  • Collection width: 60 inches (150cm)
  • Hopper capacity: 22 cubic feet
  • Twin turbine fans: 24 inches each
  • Fan speed: 3,600 RPM
  • Suction power: 1,200 CFM

Construction

  • Commercial-grade steel frame
  • Reinforced collection deck with wear plates
  • Heavy-duty plastic hopper with steel frame
  • Stainless steel twin turbine fans
  • Industrial powder-coated finish

Power Requirements

  • Minimum tractor PTO: 25 HP
  • PTO speed: 540 RPM
  • Heavy-duty driveshaft with slip clutch
  • Dual belt drive system

Dimensions & Weight

  • Overall width: 64 inches
  • Overall length: 84 inches
  • Height: 54 inches
  • Weight: 520 lbs
  • Three-point hitch: Category 1 & 2

Design Features

Advanced Collection System

  • Dual floating collection decks
  • Independent height adjustment
  • Heavy-duty anti-scalp wheels
  • Full-width dual brush strips
  • High-lift blade compatible

Professional Features

  • Hydraulic hopper dump (standard)
  • High-dump capability (up to 72")
  • Quick-release hopper system
  • LED work lights
  • Hour meter included

Applications

The PC150 is designed for professional applications:

  • Golf Courses: Maintain fairways and rough areas efficiently
  • Sports Complexes: Professional field maintenance
  • Municipal Parks: Large-scale leaf and debris collection
  • Commercial Properties: High-volume grass collection
  • Universities & Schools: Campus-wide maintenance
  • Cemetery Maintenance: Respectful, efficient grounds care
